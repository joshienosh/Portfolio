My Projects:




1. Fake Currency Detection.

The Fake Currency Detector is a comparatitive case study analysis on different fake currencies that are used in the world. I basically learned the basics of classification and regression analysis using statistical machine learning tools. I learned and implemented K-Nearest Neighbors algorithms to produce a score for whether image of a denomination is fake or not. I used openCV to alter and convert the test image into greyscale and used a threshold to remove the noise, followed by reducing the dimension of the image. I extracted the key features inside the image and exported the vector obtained to a KNN classifier to classify as "Fake" or "Real".
